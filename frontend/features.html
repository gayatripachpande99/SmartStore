Project Features

Here is what we built for SmartStore to analyze CCTV footage.

YOLOv8 Detection

We use the YOLOv8 model to detect customers walking around the store from the camera feed.

Customer Tracking

We keep track of the same person across frames so we can draw lines showing their path through the store.

Heatmaps

We overlay a heatmap on the video so you can easily see which aisles get the most foot traffic.

Dwell Time

We measure how long people stand in specific zones to figure out where they are actually looking at products.

Movement Prediction

We trained an XGBoost model on the traffic data to guess where a customer is likely to walk next.

Automated Assistant

A Python script runs in the background to look at the traffic and dwell times, and then prints out simple suggestions on how to improve the store layout.
